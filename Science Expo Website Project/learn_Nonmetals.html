Nonmetals: Family Properities

All elements within this family share several properies. Here are the most common:

  • The Nonmetals family contains 7 elements.
  • These elements have oxidation numbers of +4 and -4.
  • The elements in this family can only exist in two forms: solids or gases.
  • This family can't conduct electricity very well.
  • Most of these elements are very brittle and aren't very durable.
