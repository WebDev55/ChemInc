Alkaline Earth Metals: Family Properities

All elements within this family share several properies. Here are the most common:

  • The Alkaline Earth Metals family contains 6 elements.
  • Each of these elements have a +2 oxidation number.
  • All of these elements are extremely reactive.
  • These elements explode when they come in contact with water.
  • Most of these elements are not found in nature(in their pure forms, that is.)
  • All of these elements in this family have two electrons in their outer shell.
  • Only one of the elements in this family are radioactive.
