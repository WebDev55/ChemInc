Lanthanides: Family Properities

All elements within this family share several properies. Here are the most common:

  • The Lanthanides family contains 15 elements.
  • These elements have oxidation numbers of +3.
  • Also called the "Rare Earth Metals".
  • Used very widely in technology - most devices today contain a hint of Rare Earth Metals.
  • Although they are called the Rare Earth Metals, they are actually very abundunt, they are just hard to extract.
