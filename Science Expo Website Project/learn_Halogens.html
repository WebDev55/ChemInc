Halogens: Family Properities

All elements within this family share several properies. Here are the most common:

  • The Halogens family contains 5 elements.
  • Each of these elements have a -1 oxidation number.
  • These elements are known for forming salt.
  • This entire family is electronegative(the tendency of an atom to attract electrons to itself.)
  • Are extremely reactive, espically with the Alkali Metals and the Alkaline Earth Metals.
  • None of the family is radioactive.
